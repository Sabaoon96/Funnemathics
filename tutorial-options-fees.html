Tutorials

Tuition sessions are usually an hour long.

Though we tutor on a one to one basis, we do provide group tuition’s at the tutor’s home. Our charges are reasonable and based on factors such as:
  • How often you require tuition.
  • How far the tutor has to travel if the student wants to be tutored at his/her own home.
  • Whether it is a one-on-one or a group session.
